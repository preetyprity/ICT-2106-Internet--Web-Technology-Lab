Divisions of Bangladesh

Dhaka is the capital division of Bangladesh.

Chattogram is a major coastal city and commercial center.

Khulna is known for the Sundarbans and the Mongla port.

Rajshahi is known for education and mangoes.

Barishal is known for its rivers and rice production.

Sylhet is famous for tea gardens and natural beauty.

Rangpur is in the north-western part of Bangladesh.

Mymensingh is known for its agricultural university.
